About Me

Moses Mcknight discovered his love for programming while studying at the University of West Florida. "I signed up for a c++ programming class and was instantly hooked. The ability use code to control a computer felt like magic." It was around this time that Moses also discovered Web Development. "I've always be a creative person. Developing for the Web gives me the ability to blend my love for art with technology. It's the perfect mix." When not working on Websites, Moses enjoys traveling, photography, and the never ending hunt for the perfect cup of coffee.
